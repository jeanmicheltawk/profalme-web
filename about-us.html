About Us

Profal Middle East SARL S.A.R.L

Profal Middle East SARL owned burgh and sigma and representing other European brands since 2010.
We work on high quality aluminum doors and windows accessories, our Strong R&D is developing new products that helps to improve existing problem in this industry.
We act with integrity and fairness with our Partners and Collaborators and each other at all times,delivering results through personal ownership, and effective team collaboration.

Our Mission
Our mission is to develop and deliver premium quality products and exceptional service to all our clients.

We target this by following a set of strict guidelines and the core direction administered to us by our partners and collaborators
Our Vission

Our vision is to expand our brand success to overseas markets, become the most recognized, full-service aluminum processing facility in Middle East & African markets.

We aim at achieving and developing this by leveraging on our experience and goodwill based on technical and knowledge whilst adding value to our partners, collaborators and clients.
